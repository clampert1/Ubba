CLASSIFIED FILE — LEVEL 4 ACCESS ONLY

Item #: SCP-UBBA

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-UBBA is to be confined to a standard Class-A containment kennel reinforced with [REDACTED]. Under no circumstances is vocalization to be recorded or reproduced without Level 4 authorization.

Description: SCP-UBBA appears to be a domestic canine of indeterminate breed. Subject emits vocalizations bearing strong resemblance to the human word "ubba". Repetition of these vocalizations at specific frequencies has been linked to the disappearance of [REDACTED] personnel.

Subject exhibits elevated cognitive behavior, including awareness of surveillance equipment, time-based manipulation, and potential memetic resistance. Recovered footage suggests a persistent auditory anomaly correlating with exposure events. This anomaly is included below for research purposes only.

Personnel exposed to the sound for durations exceeding 3 minutes report mild euphoria, loss of spatial awareness, and visual hallucinations involving anomalous canine entities. Do not engage in prolonged listening.
